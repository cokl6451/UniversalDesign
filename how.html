Universal Design

How Do I Design Universally?

Steps
Step Process
Visibility Design the environment so that users are informed of what steps they can take at a given point. Predictable interactions create trust.
Feedback Users should recieve feedback when they interact in an environment.
Affordance How it looks vs. How it's used. Design an environment intuitive enough that users know how to accomplish what they want just by looking at the interface.
Mapping Controls should have a clear mapping to their effect.
Mapping Controls should have a clear mapping to their effect.
Constraints Restrict user interaction with an interface. Too much freedom has the negative effect of overwhelming the user.
Consistency For patterns to be recognized by users, it's important that the actions they take always have the same effect. Buttons should not look different on different pages and in different places.
